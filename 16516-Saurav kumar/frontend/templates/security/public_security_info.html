Stay Safe Online: Cyber Security Tips

  • Use strong, unique passwords for every account. Change them regularly.
  • Never share your OTP or password with anyone, not even officials.
  • Beware of phishing emails and suspicious links. Always check the sender's address.
  • Report cyber crimes immediately using the complaint portal or local authorities.
  • Keep your software updated to protect against the latest threats.
  • Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible.
  • Do not download unknown attachments or click on pop-ups from untrusted sources.

How to Report a Cyber Crime

  1. Collect all evidence (screenshots, emails, messages, etc.).
  2. Go to the Submit Complaint page and fill out the form.
  3. Contact your local police station if the matter is urgent.
  4. For national cyber crime reporting, visit cybercrime.gov.in.

Need Help?

Email our support team at support@crimeportal.com or call your local police helpline.
