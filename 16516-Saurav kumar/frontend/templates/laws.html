Major Criminal Laws

Indian Penal Code (IPC), 1860

The main criminal code of India covering all substantive aspects of criminal law. It defines various crimes and their punishments.

Procedural law for administration of criminal justice in India. Governs investigation, trial, and punishment of crimes.

Legal framework for electronic governance and cyber crimes. Covers digital signatures, cyber terrorism, and data protection.
