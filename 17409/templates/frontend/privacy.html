Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 28, 2025

Welcome to FitZone Gym. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring your experience with us is secure and trustworthy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information from you:

  • Personal Identification Data: Full name, email address, phone number, address, gender, date of birth, etc.
  • Health and Fitness Data: Weight, fitness goals, body measurements (if provided), and membership preferences.
  • Payment Information: Credit/debit card details or payment gateway data (processed securely).
  • Technical Information: IP address, browser type, device details, access time, and referring websites.
  • Communication Data: Inquiries, feedback, or any communication you have with us via email or forms.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data to:

  • Register your membership and manage your account.
  • Send important service updates, billing information, or appointment reminders.
  • Customize training programs based on your goals.
  • Improve our services, facilities, and user experience.
  • Send promotional offers, newsletters, and fitness tips (with your consent).

3. Sharing Your Data

We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties. However, we may share it with:

  • Trusted service providers (e.g., payment processors, CRM tools, email marketing platforms).
  • Government or legal authorities when required by law.
  • Our internal team including trainers and nutritionists (for service personalization).

4. Use of Cookies

Cookies are small files stored on your browser to enhance user experience. We use them to:

  • Understand website traffic and user behavior.
  • Store user preferences and login sessions.
  • Deliver relevant ads and promotions.

You may choose to disable cookies in your browser settings.

5. Data Security

We implement strict security measures to protect your personal data, including:

  • SSL encryption for data transmission
  • Secure payment gateways
  • Restricted access to sensitive data
  • Regular audits and updates to security protocols

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and review the information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Opt-out from receiving marketing communications

To exercise your rights, contact us at privacy@fitzonegym.com.

7. Children' s Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally to reflect changes in legal requirements or our practices. Any updates will be posted here with a revised effective date.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about our privacy policy, please contact:
