Getting Started

How do I get started with LibraTech?

+

Getting started with LibraTech is simple! Follow these steps:

  1. Sign up for a free trial: Visit our registration page and create your account
  2. Complete your profile: Add your library information and preferences
  3. Import your data: Use our bulk import tools to add your existing book catalog
  4. Invite your team: Add staff members and set up their roles and permissions
  5. Start using: Begin managing your library with our intuitive interface

Our onboarding team will guide you through each step and provide personalized training to ensure you get the most out of LibraTech.

What are the system requirements for LibraTech?

+

LibraTech is a cloud-based solution, so there are minimal system requirements:

  • Web Browser: Chrome 90+, Firefox 88+, Safari 14+, or Edge 90+
  • Internet Connection: Stable broadband connection (minimum 1 Mbps)
  • Device: Any modern computer, tablet, or smartphone
  • Screen Resolution: Minimum 1024x768 (responsive design adapts to smaller screens)

No software installation required - just access LibraTech through your web browser!

How long does the setup process take?

+

The setup process typically takes 1-3 days depending on your library size:

  • Small libraries (under 1,000 books): 1 day
  • Medium libraries (1,000-10,000 books): 2-3 days
  • Large libraries (10,000+ books): 3-5 days

This includes data migration, staff training, and system configuration. Our team works closely with you to ensure a smooth transition.

Do you provide training and support during setup?

+

Yes! We provide comprehensive training and support during your setup:

  • Dedicated onboarding specialist assigned to your library
  • Live training sessions for all staff members
  • Video tutorials and step-by-step guides
  • 24/7 support during the transition period
  • Follow-up sessions after go-live to address any questions

We're committed to ensuring your team feels confident using LibraTech from day one.

What happens to my data if I cancel my subscription?

+

Your data is always yours. Here's what happens when you cancel:

  • Data Export: You can export all your data in standard formats (CSV, JSON, MARC)
  • Grace Period: 30-day grace period to access and download your data
  • No Data Deletion: We don't immediately delete your data upon cancellation
  • Reactivation: You can reactivate your account within 90 days
  • Permanent Deletion: Data is permanently deleted after 90 days (with confirmation)

We provide detailed instructions for data export and can assist with the process if needed.
