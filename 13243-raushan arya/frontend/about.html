Our Story

Founded in 2020 by a team of passionate developers and library professionals, LibraTech emerged from a simple observation: libraries, the cornerstone of knowledge and learning, were struggling with outdated, inefficient management systems that couldn't keep up with modern demands.

Our founders, Alex Johnson (former library director) and Sarah Chen (full-stack developer), came together with a shared vision to modernize library operations. What started as a small startup in a garage has grown into a trusted partner for hundreds of libraries worldwide.

Today, we've helped transform traditional libraries into modern, efficient knowledge centers that can better serve their communities. Our platform has evolved from a simple book management system into a comprehensive library ecosystem that includes advanced analytics, mobile applications, and AI-powered features.

We're proud to serve libraries across 25+ countries, from small community libraries to large university systems, helping them manage millions of books and serve countless readers every day.
