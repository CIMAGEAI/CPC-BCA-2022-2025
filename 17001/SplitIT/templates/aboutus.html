About Us

Empowering smarter group expense management.

Welcome to SplitIT – a platform designed to simplify and streamline how you share and settle expenses with your friends, family, and colleagues.

Our mission is to eliminate the awkwardness and hassle of managing shared costs. Whether you're traveling with friends, living with roommates, or splitting a gift, SplitIT ensures everyone pays their fair share, with total transparency.

Built with cutting-edge web technology, SplitIT enables:

  • Real-time expense tracking
  • Group creation and member management
  • Accurate settlement summaries
  • Secure authentication and profile handling

Our Story: The idea of SplitIT was born from everyday frustration—unbalanced bills, forgotten contributions, and confusion over who owes what. We believe that managing money together should be simple and stress-free.

Meet the Team: We're a group of passionate students and developers committed to building tools that make life easier. We combine creativity, code, and a love for collaboration to make SplitIT the most user-friendly expense-sharing solution.

Looking Ahead: Our future plans include mobile apps, integrations with payment gateways, analytics, and smart budgeting tools. We're just getting started!

Thank you for being a part of the SplitIT community!
